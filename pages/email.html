Dash Club -2022 Edition

Hello Dash Club and Happy 2020

It has been a while, and we've got lots of good stuff from the last few months to share.

We spent the fall working on page load performance:

1. Previously, we loaded all of the JavaScript from all of the component librar- ies upfront. Now, we load them on demand. This is called “lazy loading.” So, if your home page doesn't display a graph, then the plotly.js library isn't loaded until you navigate to the page that does.

2. We started caching your app’s JavaScript and CSS assets.

With these improvements, our documentation (https:dash.plot.ly) loads much, much faster. Your apps should too!
